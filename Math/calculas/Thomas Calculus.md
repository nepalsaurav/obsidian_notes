
## Unit : Limits and Derivatives

**Secant Line:** A line that passes through two points on a curve, $P$ and $Q$.

**Tangent Line:** As you move point Q closer and close to P (until the distance between them is essentially zero), the secant line become tangent line


**Definition of Limit**

$\lim_{x \to a} f(x) = L$

Limit of  $f(x)$ , as a x approaches a, equal L.

If we make value of x arbitrarily close to L (as close to L as we like) by taking x to be sufficiently close to a (on either side of a) but not equal to a.
